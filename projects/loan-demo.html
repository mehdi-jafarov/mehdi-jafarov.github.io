Try It Yourself

This interactive model demonstrates predictive analytics using historical SBA loan data. Built for practice with limited features, it tends to favor real-estate-backed loans, reflecting historical lower-risk patterns.

The model is part of my SBA Default Risk Prediction project, which was inspired by the article Should This Loan Be Approved or Denied? .

Denies: $100.000, $40.000, Other
Approves: $100.000, $20.000, Real Estate
